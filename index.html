Our Story

A family trade, three decades deep.

Floros Plumbing & Heating was started in 1995 with one truck, one master plumber, and one rule that's never changed: do the job you'd want done in your own basement. Three decades later, we're still family-run, still answer our own phone, and still show up when we say we will.

We earned our reputation on the unglamorous stuff — the 2 a.m. burst pipes, the water heater that took out the laundry room on a Sunday, the slow drip behind the wall that nobody else could find. That work is why Angie's List members across New England voted us into the #1 plumber spot. We don't take it lightly.

Our specialty is hot water heaters — tanks, tankless, hybrid heat pumps. We also do full-service plumbing and hydronic heating (boilers, radiators, radiant floors) for residential and commercial. What we do NOT do is forced-air HVAC, AC, or duct work. What we do, we do better than anyone, and we'll be straight with you when a job is outside our lane.

If you need an honest quote, a second opinion, or a plumber who's still going to be in business in ten years to back up the work — that's what we built this company to be.
